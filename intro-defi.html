Course Overview

This course introduces the foundations of DeFi and provides a practical overview of the main decentralized applications. It is designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of DeFi and its implications for the future of finance.

 

What You'll Learn

  • - Articulate the history and origins of decentralized finance
  • - Define the key components of decentralized finance's infrastructure
  • - Explain the problems decentralized finance is designed to solve and how
  • - Identify myths or common misconceptions about decentralized finance
 

Skills You'll Gain

  • - Smart Contract
  • - Cryptography
  • - Governance
  • - Decentralized Protocols
  • - Blockchain Mechanics
 

Details to Know

  • Assessments: 4 quizzes
  • Course: Gain insight into a topic and learn the fundamentals
  • Level: Beginner
  • Recommended Experience: None
  • Duration: 1 hour (approximately)
  • Schedule: Flexible, learn at your own pace
