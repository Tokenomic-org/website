Workshop Overview

This hands-on workshop will explore a variety of products and services within the DeFi ecosystem, focusing on lending and borrowing platforms. By the end of the workshop, you will have practical experience in using these platforms to lend and borrow crypto assets, along with an understanding of the associated risks and benefits.

 

What You'll Learn

  • Understand the basics of lending and borrowing in DeFi
  • Explore various lending and borrowing platforms
  • Learn how to lend and borrow crypto assets
  • Comprehend the risks and benefits associated with DeFi lending and borrowing
 

Skills You'll Gain

  • DeFi Lending Basics
  • DeFi Borrowing Techniques
  • Risk Management
  • Crypto Asset Management
  • Understanding DeFi Protocols
 

Details to Know

  • Assessments: 2 practical exercises
  • Workshop Type: Interactive, hands-on learning
  • Level: Beginner
  • Recommended Experience: Basic understanding of cryptocurrencies
  • Duration: 3 hours (approximately)
  • Schedule: Fixed, instructor-led session

Workshop Modules

Module 1: Introduction to DeFi Lending and Borrowing

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module introduces the fundamental concepts of lending and borrowing within the DeFi ecosystem, including how these processes work and their importance in DeFi.

Content Included:

  • - 3 videos (Total 30 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 15 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Understanding lending and borrowing concepts (Total 15 minutes)

Module 2: Exploring Lending Platforms

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module covers the process of lending crypto assets on various platforms. It includes an overview of popular lending platforms, step-by-step guides, and practical exercises.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Lending crypto assets (Total 30 minutes)

Module 3: Exploring Borrowing Platforms

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module explains how to borrow crypto assets on various platforms. It provides a walkthrough of borrowing processes, along with practical tips and security practices.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 15 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 5 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Borrowing crypto assets (Total 10 minutes)

Module 4: Understanding Risks and Benefits

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module covers the risks and benefits associated with lending and borrowing in DeFi. It includes strategies for risk management and maximizing benefits.

Content Included:

  • 2 videos (Total 20 minutes)
  • 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)

Conclusion and Q&A

Duration: 30 minutes

Session Details: Open floor for participants to ask questions, discuss their experiences, and receive feedback from the instructor.
