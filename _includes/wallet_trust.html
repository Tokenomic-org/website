Why Connect a Wallet?

Your wallet is your identity in DeFi. Connecting it allows Tokenomic to personalize your learning experience based on your actual on-chain activity — no passwords, no email signups, no custodial risk.

Connect Wallet
Read-Only Access

We never request transaction signing permissions. Your wallet data is read-only — we analyze public on-chain data to build your DeFi profile.

Non-Custodial

We never hold, store, or have access to your private keys or funds. You maintain full custody of your assets at all times.

Compatible Wallets

Works with MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and any EVM-compatible browser wallet. Institutional custody solutions (Fireblocks, Ledger) are also supported.
