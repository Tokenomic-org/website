How It Works

From wallet connection to certification — a structured onboarding journey built for institutional teams entering DeFi.
01

Connect Wallet

Securely connect your Web3 wallet. We only read public on-chain data — no transaction signing required.
02

Get Your DeFi Profile

Your on-chain activity is analyzed to determine experience level, risk exposure, and personalized learning needs.
03

Access Curriculum

Get a tailored learning path covering tokenomics, risk management, governance, and institutional DeFi strategy.
04

Track Progress

Monitor your team's learning with real-time dashboards, leaderboards, and completion analytics across modules.
05

Earn Certification

Complete courses to earn verifiable credentials tied to your wallet identity. Prove competency on-chain.
