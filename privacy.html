1. Introduction

Tokenomic ("we," "our," or "us") operates the tokenomic.org platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our institutional DeFi education platform.

2. Information We Collect

Wallet Data: When you connect a Web3 wallet, we read your public wallet address. We do not access, store, or control your private keys or funds. All on-chain data we access is publicly available.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics about how you interact with the platform, including pages visited, courses accessed, and features used.

Contact Information: If you submit a contact form or book a consultation, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, organization).

3. How We Use Information

  • To personalize your learning experience based on on-chain activity
  • To track course progress and issue certifications
  • To process USDC payments on Base network
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To communicate about courses, events, and platform updates

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Wallet connections are non-custodial — we never hold or have access to your private keys. Payment transactions are processed on-chain via auditable smart contracts on Base L2.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for data storage, Base (Coinbase L2) for on-chain transactions, and standard analytics tools. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

6. Your Rights

You can disconnect your wallet at any time to stop sharing your wallet address. You may request deletion of any personal data we hold by contacting us at info@tokenomic.org.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@tokenomic.org.
