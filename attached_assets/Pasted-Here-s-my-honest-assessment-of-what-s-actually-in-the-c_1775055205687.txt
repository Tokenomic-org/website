Trust signals — No testimonials, case studies, partner logos, or outcome metrics anywhere. Even placeholder institutional proof ("Reduce DeFi onboarding time by 70%") would help.

Course progression UI — Courses are described in static pages but there's no interactive progress tracking, module completion, or "Continue where you left off" experience inside the dashboard.
