Workshop Overview

This hands-on workshop will guide you through the process of setting up a crypto wallet, buying and selling cryptocurrencies, and navigating both decentralized (DEX) and centralized exchanges (CEX). By the end of the workshop, you will have practical experience in managing crypto assets and using various exchange platforms.

What You'll Learn

  • - Set up and secure a crypto wallet
  • - Buy and sell cryptocurrencies
  • - Navigate and trade on decentralized exchanges (DEX)
  • - Navigate and trade on centralized exchanges (CEX)
  • - Understand key security practices for managing crypto assets
 

Skills You'll Gain

  • - Crypto Wallet Setup
  • - Cryptocurrency Trading
  • - Exchange Navigation (DEX & CEX)
  • - Security Best Practices
  • - Basic Blockchain Transactions
 

Details to Know

  • Assessments: 5 practical exercises
  • Workshop Type: Interactive, hands-on learning
  • Level: Beginner
  • Recommended Experience: Basic understanding of cryptocurrencies
  • Duration: 3 hours (approximately)
  • Schedule: Fixed, instructor-led session
 

Workshop Modules

Module 1: Introduction and Crypto Wallet Setup

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module introduces the basics of crypto wallets, including different types (hot vs. cold), and guides you through setting up and securing your first crypto wallet.

Content Included:

  • - 1 video (Total 10 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Setting up a crypto wallet (Total 30 minutes)

Module 2: Buying and Selling Cryptocurrencies

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module covers the process of buying and selling cryptocurrencies. It includes an overview of popular platforms, step-by-step guides, and practical exercises.

Content Included:

  • - 1 video (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Buying and selling cryptocurrencies (Total 30 minutes)

Module 3: Navigating Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module explains what centralized exchanges are, how they work, and provides a walkthrough of using a popular CEX. It includes practical tips for trading and security practices.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 15 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 5 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Trading on a centralized exchange (Total 10 minutes)

Module 4: Navigating Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module introduces decentralized exchanges, explains how they differ from centralized exchanges, and provides a practical guide to using a popular DEX. It includes key concepts like liquidity pools and slippage.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 15 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 5 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Trading on a decentralized exchange (Total 10 minutes)

Module 5: Security Best Practices

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: The final module covers essential security practices for managing crypto assets, including tips for avoiding scams, securing your wallet, and safeguarding your private keys.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)
 

Conclusion and Q&A

Duration: 30 minutes

Session Details: Open floor for participants to ask questions, discuss their experiences, and receive feedback from the instructor.

Workshop Wrap-Up

  • - Next steps for continued learning and practice
  • - Resources for further exploration
