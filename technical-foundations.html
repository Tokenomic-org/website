--- layout: page title: "Technical Foundations Of Blockchain & DeFi" --- # Technical Foundations Of Blockchain & DeFi ### Course Overview **Technical Foundations Of Blockchain & DeFi** This course offers an in-depth technical understanding of the technologies that underpin DeFi, providing a comprehensive exploration of blockchain technology, smart contracts, and decentralized applications. ### What You'll Learn - Articulate the fundamental principles of blockchain technology - Explain how smart contracts function and their role in DeFi - Define the key components of decentralized applications (dApps) - Identify technical challenges and solutions in the DeFi space ### Skills You'll Gain - Blockchain Architecture - Smart Contract Development - Cryptographic Techniques - Decentralized Application Design - Consensus Algorithms ### Details to Know - **Assessments:** 4 quizzes - **Course:** Gain advanced insights into blockchain and DeFi technologies - **Level:** Intermediate - **Recommended Experience:** Basic understanding of blockchain and DeFi concepts - **Duration:** 6 hours (approximately) - **Schedule:** Flexible, learn at your own pace ### Course Modules **Module 1: Blockchain Fundamentals** - **Duration:** 1 hour - **Module Details:** This module covers the foundational concepts of blockchain technology, including its architecture, key components, and consensus mechanisms.
