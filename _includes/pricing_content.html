How do I pay with USDC?+
Connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask or Rabby) and ensure you have USDC on the Base L2 network. Payments are processed through Helio with near-zero gas fees.
Can I get a refund?+
Refunds are handled case-by-case. If unsatisfied within the first 7 days and less than 20% complete, contact info@tokenomic.org for a full refund.
What are certification NFTs?+
Upon completing a course, Professional and Institution members receive a soulbound NFT on Base chain that verifies completion, permanently linked to your wallet.
Do you offer team discounts?+
Yes. The Institution plan includes volume pricing for teams of 10+. Contact sales for a custom quote.
Why USDC on Base instead of traditional payment?+
Base L2 offers fees under $0.01, instant settlement, and full on-chain transparency. USDC means stable pricing without crypto volatility.
Can I switch plans later?+
Absolutely. Upgrade from Explorer to Professional at any time. Progress and community memberships carry over. Mid-year upgrades are prorated.
