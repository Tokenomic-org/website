Workshop Overview

This hands-on workshop will guide you through the process of setting up an NFT wallet, minting your first NFT, buying and trading NFTs on various platforms, and understanding NFT copyrights and licensing. By the end of the workshop, you will have practical experience in managing NFT assets and participating in the NFT marketplace.

What You'll Learn

  • - Set up and secure an NFT wallet
  • - Mint your first NFT
  • - Buy and trade NFTs on various platforms
  • - Understand NFT copyrights and licensing
 

Skills You'll Gain

  • - NFT Wallet Setup
  • - NFT Minting
  • - NFT Trading
  • - Understanding NFT Copyrights and Licensing
  • - Basic Blockchain Transactions
 

Details to Know

  • Assessments: 2 practical exercises
  • Workshop Type: Interactive, hands-on learning
  • Level: Beginner
  • Recommended Experience: Basic understanding of blockchain and NFTs
  • Duration: 3 hours (approximately)
  • Schedule: Fixed, instructor-led session
 

Workshop Modules

Module 1: Introduction and NFT Wallet Setup

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module introduces the basics of NFT wallets, including different types (hot vs. cold), and guides you through setting up and securing your first NFT wallet.

Content Included:

  • - 3 videos (Total 30 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Setting up an NFT wallet (Total 30 minutes)

Module 2: Minting Your First NFT

Duration: 1 hour

Module Details: This module covers the process of minting your first NFT. It includes an overview of popular platforms, step-by-step guides, and practical exercises.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Minting your first NFT (Total 30 minutes)

Module 3: Buying and Trading NFTs

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module explains how to buy and trade NFTs on various platforms. It provides a walkthrough of using popular NFT marketplaces, along with practical tips for trading and security practices.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 15 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 5 minutes)
  • - 1 hands-on exercise: Trading NFTs on a marketplace (Total 10 minutes)

Module 4: Understanding NFT Copyrights and Licensing

Duration: 30 minutes

Module Details: This module covers essential knowledge about NFT copyrights and licensing, including tips for avoiding legal issues and understanding the rights of NFT creators and buyers.

Content Included:

  • - 2 videos (Total 20 minutes)
  • - 1 reading (Total 10 minutes)

Conclusion and Q&A

Duration: 30 minutes

Session Details: Open floor for participants to ask questions, discuss their experiences, and receive feedback from the instructor.

Workshop Wrap-Up

  • - Next steps for continued learning and practice
