Privacy Policy for Tokenomic.org

Effective Date: April 2, 2026 | Last Updated: April 2, 2026

At Tokenomic.org, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our community members—including institutional learners, educators, and consultants. As a decentralized, community-led platform, we prioritize data sovereignty and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information across our website, dashboard, and educational modules.

1. The Web3 Data Paradigm

Unlike traditional platforms, Tokenomic.org operates on a "Privacy by Design" framework.

  • Public Blockchain Data: Your interactions with smart contracts (e.g., course enrollments, certificate minting, or payment splits) are recorded on public ledgers (e.g., Ethereum, Solana, or Layer 2s). This data is immutable and public by nature.
  • Self-Sovereign Identity: We encourage the use of non-custodial wallets (e.g., MetaMask, Rabby). We do not store your private keys or seed phrases.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a tailored institutional experience, we collect:

  • Wallet Addresses: Used as your primary identifier for access control and progress tracking.
  • Profile Data: Voluntarily provided information such as professional bio, expertise tags (e.g., DeFi Architect, Legal Counsel), and profile images.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data regarding how you interact with our "Modeling Labs" and "Expert Marketplace" to improve UI/UX.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us for advisory services or support, we retain record of that correspondence.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to:

  • Facilitate Community Offerings: Connect institutional learners with relevant consultant-led workshops and research.
  • Manage Revenue Splits: Ensure the 90/5/5 revenue model is accurately executed via on-chain protocols.
  • AI Personalization: Use local or privacy-preserving AI to recommend educational modules based on your previous activity.
  • Compliance: Meet institutional "Know Your Business" (KYB) requirements where applicable for high-level advisory.

4. Third-Party Integrations

We use verified Web3 infrastructure providers:

  • Payment Rails: Integration with protocols like Helio or 0xSplit for automated disbursements.
  • Storage: Decentralized storage (IPFS/Arweave) for course materials and research papers.
  • Analytics: Privacy-focused tools that do not track PII (Personally Identifiable Information).

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CPRA / Global Standards)

Regardless of your jurisdiction, Tokenomic.org grants you:

  • The Right to Access: Request a copy of the off-chain data we hold about you.
  • The Right to Deletion: Request the removal of your off-chain profile data. Note: On-chain transaction history cannot be deleted.
  • The Right to Data Portability: Export your learning progress and badges to other compatible Web3 platforms.

6. Security Measures

We employ institutional-grade security:

  • Smart Contract Audits: All code managing community funds or data is regularly audited.
  • Encryption: All off-chain communications are encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • No-Sell Policy: We never sell your data to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

7. AI Governance

In accordance with 2026 regulations (e.g., EU AI Act), we disclose that AI may be used to:

  • Summarize community-led research.
  • Moderate the "Expert Marketplace" for quality assurance.
  • You have the right to opt-out of automated profiling in your Dashboard Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

As a community-led project, significant changes to this policy may be subject to a governance vote by Tokenomic.org contributors. We will notify you of any updates via the site dashboard.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights:

Email: privacy@tokenomic.org
