These values come from environment variables exposed by the Cloudflare Pages build (set under Workers & Pages -> tokenomic -> Settings -> Environment variables). They are read here at Jekyll build time and emitted as a single `window.__TKN_ENV` object that the web3 frontend reads at runtime.
